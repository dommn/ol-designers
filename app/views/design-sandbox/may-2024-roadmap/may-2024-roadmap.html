    What we're working on now

    GOV.UK One Login user activity history SIGNED IN EXPERIENCE

    Users will be able to view their GOV.UK One Login account activity history.

    Users can prove their identity without photo ID IDENTITY

    Users who cannot prove their identity with photo ID will be able to enter UK bank account details and their National Insurance number.

    Users can keep their details up to date after proving their identity IDENTITY

    Users will be able to make updates to their identity details (for example, their name) to keep their GOV.UK One Login up to date.

    Maintaining trust in users who have already proved their identity IDENTITY

    To maintain confidence levels over time, you’ll be able to repeat some identity checks for users who have already proved their identity.

    New data sources for security questions IDENTITY

    New data sources and questions will be available for users proving their identity by answering security questions online (also known as knowledge-based verification questions).

    Recently released

    More configuration controls in the self-service admin tool SERVICE TEAM ONBOARDING

    Service teams can add and change an additional three configuration attributes in the self-service admin tool. This replaces the old route of having to contact a member of the GOV.UK One Login support team.

    Also, admin tool users can now create and manage more than one service from the same account.

    Two-factor authentication recovery SIGN IN

    If users lose access to their two-factor authentication method, we’ll help them get back into their GOV.UK One Login.

    Users have other options to prove their identity if they’re not successful first time IDENTITY

    Users who are not able to prove their identity the first time can try using another ID document or a different method.

    Common navigation header for services connected to GOV.UK One Login SERVICE TEAM ONBOARDING

    Services using GOV.UK One Login will be able to use a common navigation header.

    Use an international mobile phone number to create a GOV.UK One Login Sign in

    Users can create their GOV.UK One Login using an international mobile phone number.

    Services are shown in a user's GOV.UK One Login Signed in experience

    Users can see and access all the services they’ve used with their GOV.UK One Login.

    Updates

    As we’re still at an early stage, our plans may shift. We’ll update this page when this happens and add more detail when we can.

    We’ll keep sharing in blog posts and at our regular cross-government show and tells too.
