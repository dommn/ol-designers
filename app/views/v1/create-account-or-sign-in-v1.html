About GOV.UK accounts

The GOV.UK account is new. It’s separate from other government accounts, such as Government Gateway or your personal tax account.

In the future, you’ll be able to use your GOV.UK account to sign in to all government services. For now, you need different accounts to access different government services.

We’re still building GOV.UK accounts and adding more features and services.
