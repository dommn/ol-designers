Get started with GOV.UK Sign In

1. Check if GOV.UK Sign In is right for you

GOV.UK Sign In is for central government services that need authentication.

You will also need to have development expertise in your team.

Find out more about GOV.UK Sign In.

2. Create an account to test GOV.UK Sign In

Create an account to access our test environment. This will let you see how GOV.UK Sign In would work with your service.

You need to use your gov.uk email address.

{{ govukButton({ text: "Create account", href: "create-account-or-sign-in-v1" }) }}

3. Request to join private beta

If you think GOV.UK Sign In is right for your service, you’ll be able to request to join private beta from within your GOV.UK Sign In account.

4. Go live

Once you’ve been accepted into the private beta, you’ll need to sign our memorandum of understanding.

You can then go live with support from our technical team, who will be available 24/7 for urgent incidents.

Contact us

Use our online form to report problems, ask questions or suggest improvements.

You can also get in touch via our Slack channel.
