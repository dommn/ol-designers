    • About GOV.UK One Login

    GOV.UK One Login lets your users sign into your service quickly and easily.

    It will ultimately allow users to access any government service using the same email address and password.

    The user-facing parts of GOV.UK One Login

    Users can sign in

    Users can sign in to your service via GOV.UK One Login, with their username, passport and two-factor authentication.

    You can add this functionality into your service alone - you do not have to add the identity checks if you do not need them.

    Find out more about the sign in journey.

    Users can prove their identity

    Users can prove their identity so you know they are who they say they are.

    If you want to add identity checks, you must also have the ‘sign in’ functionality.

    Find out more about identity checks.

    Users create a reusable GOV.UK One Login

    Users have a space where they can manage their GOV.UK One Login, among other things.

    This is created once a user first signs into a service with GOV.UK One Login.

    Find out more about users' signed in experience.

    User support

    Support for your users is available during office hours via a support form. We aim to reply within 2 working days.

    Features for your service team

    Documentation

    You can follow our documentation to:

    • understand the OpenID Connect (OIDC) authentication protocol we're using
    • see how user data is shared
    • test GOV.UK One Login with your service in our integration environment

    Support

    Support for your service is available during office hours via a support form and Slack channel.

    If you go live with GOV.UK One Login, our technical team is available 24/7 for urgent incidents.
